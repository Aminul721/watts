Mixed Flow Blower

The Cook TMX: Mixed Flow Blower is designed for supply, exhaust, or return air applications. Performance ranges from 410 to 24,400 CFM with static pressures to 3” w.g. The TMX is offered in 12 sizes from 90 to 330. With the Cook Contour® axial-centrifugal hybrid impeller, these mixed-flow fans are more efficient than traditional centrifugal units. This provides you the opportunity to reduce motor horsepower and fan sound levels while using a smaller unit.

Ruskin Critical Environment (CE) Damper Webinar

Ruskin’s Critical Environment (CE) damper series offers products suited for corrosive prone, or sanitary based applications. The critical environment damper series is designed and manufactured with all the necessary features required to withstand the elements of coastal and corrosive applications. These models combine the strength of stainless steel and the protection of anodized aluminum to combat salt water elements.

This Ruskin Webinar will cover the product names and damper type, features and options that make them durable, and suitable applications.
